Personal Interests | Highlight of Personal & Professional Work


One of Annette’s personal interests is playing volleyball. During her free time, she often goes and plays volleyball with the community or organizes volleyball runs with her friends. She was a 4 year varsity player in High School and continues to play for UTM these past two years. Her love and passion for volleyball has also encouraged her to organize her own volleyball camps with her friends for Middle School and Secondary School students.

Annette’s interest and passion in photography has led to the majority of her personal and professional projects utilizing photography to highlight elements and contexts of her work. She has also participated in multiple clubs and student led organizations that allow her to continue to build on her experience in photography and graphic design.

Her capstone project was a travel/history book that introduced readers to the food, culture, and history of Taiwan. Annette traveled to 5 different cities within 3 months to interview local residents, explore historical sites, and try out local cuisine. This was all to demonstrate how food, culture, and history interlace with each other to uniquely shape each city.
